Respond immediately with RexArmy, built off industry-standard Crowdstrike Orchestra technology, to kill an attack the moment it starts. With Rex's suite, your AI security team is ready at the blink of an eye. Whether it's 2 AM or 12 PM, RexArmy shuts down threats instantly.
